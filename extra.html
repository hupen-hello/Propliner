M3M Jacob & Co. Residences is a luxury residential development by M3M India, situated in Sector 97, Noida. Spanning 6 acres of prime land ,it features four towers, offering ground-level access to thirty-four floors. The project comprises 250 meticulously designed spacious units, available in 3, 4, and 5-BHK configurations. This is India's first-ever project by Jacob and Co., which M3M India launched. The project is surrounded by 300 acres of lush land, offering a view of the Yamuna River and a golf course, which creates the perfect blend of luxury and comfort for residents.

Strategically set, M3M Jacob & Co Residences offers the best connectivity to Noida's key destinations, including Noida-Greater Noida Expressway, Golf Course Metro Station, Akshardham Temple, and many more, making the project well-suited for tenants, families, and professionals. M3M Jacob & Co Residences is a newly launched project, with possession scheduled to begin in July 2030. Additionally, the payment plan for this project is flexible, making decision-making easier. Interested buyers and investors can request the M3M Jacob & Co. Residences cost sheet for a complete breakdown and pricing details.
